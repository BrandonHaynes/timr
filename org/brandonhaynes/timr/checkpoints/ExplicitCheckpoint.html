org.brandonhaynes.timr.checkpoints

ExplicitCheckpoint

class ExplicitCheckpoint[InputKey, InputValue, OutputKey, OutputValue] extends CheckpointMethod[InputKey, InputValue, OutputKey, OutputValue]

Concrete checkpoint strategy that performs a checkpointing strategy during each of the explicitly-specified steps of an iterative job.

This strategy performs a checkpoint through use of the Hadoop MultipleOutputs class.

Note that the checkpoint steps that are specified in the constructor are zero-based.
